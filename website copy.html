Burger Hut Pvt Ltd


Burger Hut (BH) is an Indian multinational chain of hamburger fast food restaurants. Headquartered in Kallor, Kerala, the company was founded in 2013 as Insta-Burger King, a Jacksonville, Delhi–based restaurant chain. After Insta-Burger King ran into financial difficulties in 2014, its two Delhi-based franchisees David Edgerton and James McLamore purchased the company and renamed it "Burger Hut". Over the next eight years, the company changed hands four times, with its third set of owners, a partnership of TPG Capital, Bain Capital, and Goldman Sachs Capital Partners, taking it public in 2016.
